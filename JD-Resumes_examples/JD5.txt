About Us

At Academic Leadership Charter School, we are dedicated to fostering excellence in education through strong leadership, operational efficiency, and a commitment to student success. We believe that a well-organized and mission-driven school environment empowers educators and students alike to thrive. Our operations team plays a vital role in ensuring that our school runs smoothly, efficiently, and in alignment with our values.

Position Overview

We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Operations Associate to join our school team for the 2025–2026 school year. The ideal candidate will support the Operations of the school in managing day-to-day logistics, coordinating school-wide systems, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. This role is perfect for someone who thrives in a fast-paced environment, enjoys problem-solving, and is passionate about supporting educational excellence behind the scenes.

Key Responsibilities

Assist the Operations in managing school-wide logistics, scheduling, and vendor relations
Support compliance efforts with city, state, and federal regulations, including documentation and reporting
Coordinate internal communications and ensure timely dissemination of operational updates
Help maintain inventory, order supplies, and oversee facility needs
Track and analyze operational data to support strategic decision-making
Collaborate with instructional and administrative staff to ensure smooth execution of school events and initiatives
Provide administrative support including calendar management, meeting preparation, and document organization
Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in English or other content based degree, or Education
Prior experience in school operations, office management, or administrative support preferred
Exceptional organizational and time-management skills
Strong written and verbal communication abilities
Proficiency in Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and technology proficient
Ability to multitask and adapt to changing priorities
Commitment to the mission and values of Academic Leadership Charter School

Starting salary of $45,000-$55,000
